Changwon

This is my hometown. Although I was not born here, I am attached to it as much as I have spent a long time. The population is over 1 million and it is quite a big city.

Location
It was located in the southeastern tip of Korea and next to Busan. Also, the sea is adjacent.

Attractions

Navy port festival
The navy port festival, also known as the Cherry Blossom Festival, has been a memorial service since 1952, and is held every summer at Jinhae Naval Base. At the festival, you can enjoy numerous cherry blossoms and military band performances.

Anmingogae Pass
This is the place I used to go often because of the beautiful scenery. Because it is located in the mountain, the air is good and the road is pretty, so it is good to ride a bicycle or walk
